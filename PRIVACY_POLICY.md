# Privacy Policy for Block Jam Master

**Last Updated: March 2024**

Welcome to Block Jam Master. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

### 1. Data Collection
We collect minimal data to improve your experience. This includes:
*   High scores and level progress.
*   In-game currency (Coins) balance.
*   Game settings (Sound/Vibration preferences).
This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our private servers.

### 2. Advertising
We use third-party advertising partners (such as Google AdMob) to serve advertisements within the game. These partners may collect and use anonymous data (such as your device's Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your Android device settings.

### 3. In-App Purchases
All financial transactions for "Remove Ads" or Coin bundles are handled securely by the Google Play Store. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card or banking information.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Our game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
support@blockjammaster.com
